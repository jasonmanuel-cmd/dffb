Residential Senior Living Services

DFFB Senior Living delivers customized care options from independent living to comprehensive daily assistance, all within accessible and comfortable homes.

Independent Living

Enjoy the freedom of independent living in a supportive community, surrounded by peers and friendly staff, with optional services to enhance daily life.

Continuum of Care

Benefit from seamless transfer between care tiers as health needs evolve, ensuring the right support without relocating.

Assisted Living

Receive customized assistance with daily activities, medication management, and personal care in a safe and nurturing home-style setting.

Core Offerings

  • Long-term senior housing
  • Assisted daily living support
  • Meal preparation
  • Transportation coordination
  • Family communication support

Care Delivery Proof Points

  • Structured movement between independent and assisted support without unnecessary relocation.
  • Small-home environment designed for higher visibility on resident needs.
  • Documented support services: meals, transportation coordination, and daily living assistance.
