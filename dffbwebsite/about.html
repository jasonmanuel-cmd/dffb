Building the Future of Senior Housing in California

DFFB Senior Living, operated by DFFB Management Group LLC, provides a unique blend of affordable, home-style housing and flexible care for individuals aged 55 and over in Bakersfield.

Our approach ensures residents receive the right support as their needs evolve, whether they want to maintain independence or require hands-on assistance.

What Makes DFFB Different

By combining real estate innovation with high-quality healthcare, we offer a seamless continuum of care in familiar residential settings.

Our scalable model is built to make every stage of aging comfortable and dignified for both residents and families.

What We Combine

  • Residential living environments
  • Structured care support
  • Real estate scalability

Our Mission

Our mission at DFFB Senior Living is to empower older adults to live independently and with dignity, while providing scalable care solutions that adapt as individual needs change.

We redefine senior living by merging real estate innovation with compassionate healthcare, ensuring safety, comfort, and connection for every resident, every step of the way.
