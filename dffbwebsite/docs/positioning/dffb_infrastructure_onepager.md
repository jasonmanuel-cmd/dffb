Problem

Our Solution
DFFB provides the infrastructure layer that turns under-used residential properties into licensed, high-quality small senior homes. 3. Referral & Intake Network — Hospital, advisor, and community partnerships plus an intake engine that shortens ramp time and sustains occupancy.
