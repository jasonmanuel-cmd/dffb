Quick Answers for Families and Partners

How quickly can DFFB help with emergency placement?
DFFB responds to urgent calls and starts placement coordination as quickly as possible based on care needs and available homes in Bakersfield and Kern County.

What kind of senior living does DFFB provide?
DFFB provides residential, home-style housing with support levels that range from independent living to assisted care as resident needs change.

Does DFFB serve only Bakersfield?
DFFB is rooted in Bakersfield and Kern County and is building toward broader California expansion through a scalable care-and-housing model.

Is there an investor opportunity?
Yes. DFFB operates a recurring-revenue senior housing platform designed for multi-home expansion with real estate-backed operations.
